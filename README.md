## ✨ Features
> **Pure-Flow** isn't just a config; it's an experience. Designed for simplicity, dynamic colors, and smooth performance on Hyprland.

* **Dynamic Colors (Matugen):** Colors change instantly based on your wallpaper.
* **Glassmorphism:** Deep blur and crystal transparency in Dolphin and Waybar.
* **Clean UI:** Minimalist Rofi launcher and SwayNC notification center.
* **Performance:** Highly optimized for a lag-free, fluid interaction.
